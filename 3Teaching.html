Overview

I teach at USC as a Graduate Teaching Assistant (TA). The main responsibility as a TA in linguistics is to lead discussions and teach handy skills for language study. The courses that I have taught as a TA at USC include Language and Society and Language and Mind. I have also TA'ed for Introduction to Linguistics at East China Normal University.

I have completed various teacher training courses, including the Teacher's Education Program and the course Teaching Practicum.
