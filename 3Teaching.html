Overview

I teach at USC as a Graduate Teaching Assistant (TA). The main responsibility as a TA in linguistics is to lead discussions and teach handy skills for language study. The courses that I have taught as a TA at USC include:

  • Language and Society
  • Language and Mind

I have completed various teacher training courses, including the Teacher's Education Program (Tianjin Normal University) and Teaching Practicum (Department of Linguistics, USC).
