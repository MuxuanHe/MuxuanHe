About Me

I am Ph.D. candidate in linguistics at USC. My major interest lies in various phenomena at morphology-phonology interface, including reduplication, subtractive morphology, morphological mutation, etc. Besides formal phonology and morphology, I also work on articulatory phonology, phonetics, and under-documented languages. My experience of investigating under-studied languages is introduced here.

Currently, I am a member of USC Phonetics and Phonology Group and Language and Computation Lab (ShLAC).

(Duomo di Milano, January 2019)
